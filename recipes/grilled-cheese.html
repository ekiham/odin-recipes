Grilled cheese recipe

This is one of the better grilled cheese recipes you'll ever try!

This has to be one of my favourite recipes I've found in the past year! This is the recipe which will make everyone love you no matter what.

And it's so easy to make too. There is no hassle of processing stuff, just put the bread on a pan and toss in the cheese!

Ingredients

  • Good bread
  • High quality ham
  • Gruyere cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter your bread slices and star toasting them in the pan
  2. Once toasted on one side, flip and add cheese and ham
  3. Wait until toasted from the other side too!
  4. Enjoy!
