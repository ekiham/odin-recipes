Pancakes recipe

These are the pancakes to try!

Man these pancakes are so lovely, they absolutely melt in your mouth!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add flour, eggs, milk, sugar in the pot and mix together until silky smooth
  2. Add butter to your pan and set it on medium high
  3. Add about 1dl of batter on a pan (depending on your pan size)
  4. Fry the pancake for about two minutes per side, then flip
  5. Add sugar and cinnamon on top of the pancakes, and enjoy!
